Frequently Asked Questions
How fast will my order be delivered?
Most orders within LAUTECH campus are assigned to a rider immediately and delivered within 15-30 minutes after your payment is confirmed.
How do I know the fee for an outside-campus delivery?
When you select "Outside Campus", we will chat with you quickly on WhatsApp to agree on a custom delivery fee based on the distance before you make any secure payment.
Is my payment secure?
Yes! All payments are processed securely via Paystack. Everything is transparent and trackable right on your dashboard.
Can I order from any vendor on campus?
Absolutely. Just write down the exact canteen, restaurant, or vendor you want, and precisely what you need. Our riders will go directly there and bring it to you.
